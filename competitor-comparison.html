✅ BIVVO's Competitive Position

BIVVO wins on the #1 purchase objection — timing chip safety. Every magnetic competitor carries this risk. BIVVO eliminates it entirely with zero magnets. Combined with superior durability (500+ vs 50–200 races), competitive pricing ($14.99), and the only twist-lock mechanism at consumer scale, BIVVO has a defensible position in a growing category.

Key vulnerability: Lower brand awareness and review count at launch. Magnetic clips have 2,800+ reviews (BibBoards). BIVVO needs 50+ reviews in 90 days via Vine + organic to compete for page-1 organic rank.
