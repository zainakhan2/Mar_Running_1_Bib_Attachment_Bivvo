MCF Fulfillment

ByteStand app ($9.99/mo) bridges Shopify orders to Amazon Multi-Channel Fulfillment. Customer orders on bivvo.com → ByteStand sends to Amazon MCF → Amazon ships from FBA inventory. Cost: $4.50/unit Standard delivery (per G-05). Net DTC margin: 38.4%.

Default to STANDARD speed in ByteStand settings. Do NOT default to Expedited ($5.00–$5.50) — destroys DTC margin advantage.
