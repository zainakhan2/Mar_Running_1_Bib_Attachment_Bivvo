Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026 · Effective for bivvo.com

1. Information We Collect

When you visit bivvo.com or make a purchase, we collect:

  • Order information: Name, email, shipping address, payment info (processed by Shopify Payments — we never see your full card number).
  • Account information: Email when you sign up for newsletters.
  • Device information: IP address, browser type, OS, referring URLs via Google Analytics 4, Meta Pixel, TikTok Pixel.
  • Communications: Emails, messages, or reviews you send us.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Process and fulfill orders (shipping, payment, customer service)
  • Send transactional emails (order confirmation, shipping updates) via Omnisend
  • Send marketing emails (opt-in only) — unsubscribe anytime
  • Improve website, products, and customer experience
  • Run targeted advertising via Meta and TikTok using anonymized pixel data
  • Prevent fraud and comply with legal obligations

3. Sharing Your Information

We never sell your personal information. We share data only with: Shopify (e-commerce platform), Amazon MCF (fulfillment — receives shipping address only), Omnisend (email marketing), Google Analytics (anonymized traffic), Meta & TikTok (anonymized conversion data).

4. Cookies

We use cookies for essential functionality (cart, checkout), analytics (GA4), and advertising (Meta Pixel, TikTok Pixel). Disable in browser settings if preferred.

5. Data Retention

Order data retained for tax/legal requirements (typically 7 years). Marketing data retained until you unsubscribe or request deletion.

6. Your Rights

You can: access, correct, delete your data, opt out of marketing, or request a data copy. Email hello@bivvo.com with subject "Privacy Request."

7. California Residents (CCPA)

Right to know, right to delete, right to opt out of sale (we don't sell data), right to non-discrimination.

8. Children's Privacy

Products designed for ages 13+. We don't knowingly collect info from children under 13.
