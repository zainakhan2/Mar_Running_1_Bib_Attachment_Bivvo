Year 1 Expo Strategy

3–5 expos recommended for Year 1. Focus on fall marathon season (September–November) which has the highest concentration of races and the most motivated buyers.

Priority markets: Northeast corridor (NYC, Boston, Philadelphia — highest runner density), Chicago (major fall marathon), California (LA, SF — year-round racing).

Expo function is DUAL: (1) Direct sales revenue (10% of Year 1 total), and (2) Brand awareness + customer acquisition for Amazon/DTC channels. Every expo attendee who sees the demo becomes a potential Amazon buyer later.

Expo Preparation

  • Apply 8–12 weeks before each event (most expos have limited vendor spots)
  • Booth setup: 6' table, vertical banner, demo station with sample bibs + clips
  • Staffing: founder solo for most events; ambassador opt-in for larger expos (G-09)
  • Demo script: 15-second Insert-Twist-Click demonstration converts 3x better than verbal pitch
  • Post-expo follow-up email within 48 hours to all email signups collected
  • Full 22-item packing checklist available (PG-08 resolution)
