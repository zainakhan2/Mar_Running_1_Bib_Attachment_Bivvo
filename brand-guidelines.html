Brand Voice

Confident

✅ DO: "BIVVO clicks locked. Period." / "Zero magnets. Guaranteed chip safety."
❌ DON'T: "We think our clips might be better..." / "BIVVO could potentially work for you."

Runner-to-Runner

✅ DO: "We've dropped that third pin in wet grass too." / "Happy racing!"
❌ DON'T: "Dear valued customer" / "Our product leverages innovative synergies."

Warm & Slightly Playful

✅ DO: "The satisfying click? Chef's kiss." / "Your timing chip will thank you."
❌ DON'T: Sarcasm, put-downs, or humor at the customer's expense.

Never Corporate

✅ DO: Short sentences. Active voice. Contractions are great. Emoji sparingly 🏃
❌ DON'T: Buzzwords, jargon, passive voice, or sentences over 25 words.
