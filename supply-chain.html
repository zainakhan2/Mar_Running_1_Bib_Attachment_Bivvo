Restock Formula

Reorder Point = (Average Daily Sales × Lead Time Days) + Safety Stock

Lead time: 70 days (ocean freight + prep). Safety stock: 14 days. Example at 5 units/day:

(5 × 70) + (5 × 14) = 420 units → reorder when inventory hits 420

Monitor daily via Task #102. P6B Excel Sheet 5 (Restock Calculator) auto-calculates based on your actual daily sales rate.
