Race Day Checklist: 25 Things Every Runner Needs

~2,400 words · 9 min read · Target: "race day checklist" · Publish: Launch Day (W0)

Whether it's your first 5K or your 50th marathon, the morning-of scramble is real. Bookmark this checklist — you'll use it before every race.

The Night Before (Pack #1–10)

1
Race bib + timing chip
Attach the night before using bib clips.
2
Race shirt/singlet
Nothing new on race day.
3
Running shorts/tights
Weather-appropriate. Check forecast.
4
Running shoes
Trusted, broken-in race shoes.
5
Running socks
Anti-blister. Pack backup pair.
6
GPS watch
Charged to 100%. Race screen set.
7
Fuel (gels, chews)
Pre-counted for your distance.
8
Body Glide
Inner thighs, armpits, nipples.
9
Sunscreen SPF 30+
Apply 20 min before start.
10
Sunglasses
If facing sunrise at start.
11
Pre-race breakfast
2–3 hrs before. Tested meal only.

Race Morning (#11–20)

Pre-race breakfast
2–3 hrs before. Tested meal only.
12
Water bottle
Sip until 30 min before start.
13
Bib clips (4-pack)
12 seconds vs 60 sec with pins.
14
Throwaway warm layer
Old hoodie. Discard at start.
15
Disposable gloves
Cheap warmth. Toss at mile 1.
16
Hat or headband
Sweat control + sun protection.
17
Phone (waterproof case)
Emergency, photos, Strava.
18
Cash (0)
Parking, food, emergency Uber.
19
Post-race dry clothes
Body temp drops fast after effort.
20
Foam roller
Keep in car for post-race.
21
Confirm bib visible + secure
Officials must see your number.

At the Start Line (#21–25)

Confirm bib visible + secure
Officials must see your number.
22
Start GPS watch early
2–3 min for satellite lock.
23
Final bathroom visit
Budget 15–20 min for line.
24
Dynamic warm-up (5 min)
Leg swings, high knees, light jog.
25
Positive self-talk
You trained. You're ready. Smile.
