P1D: Timing Chip Compatibility Testing

Proving BIVVO's zero-magnet mechanism cannot interfere with RFID race timing chips.

Technical Analysis

RFID timing chips operate at 125kHz (LF) or 13.56MHz (HF). Common systems: Champion Chip (ankle transponder), MyLaps (bib-mounted or shoe-mounted), Chronotrack (D-tag on bib).

Magnetic interference requires a magnetic field > 1 gauss at chip proximity. Neodymium magnets in competitor clips generate 50–200 gauss at the clip face — well above the interference threshold.

BIVVO generates ZERO magnetic field. POM (Delrin®) is a non-ferromagnetic polymer. The bayonet-mount mechanism is purely mechanical — rotation + friction lock. No magnets, no magnetic materials, no conductive metals that could create eddy currents.
