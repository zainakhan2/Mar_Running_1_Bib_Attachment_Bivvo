P1B: Industrial Design & Engineering

2-piece bayonet-mount design — base plate, locking cap, injection mold specifications.

Design Specifications

ParameterSpecification
Assembly2-piece: base plate (female) + locking cap (male)
Diameter30mm outer diameter (industry standard for bib clips)
Height (assembled)8mm total stack height (low profile)
Locking mechanism3 bayonet lugs, 90° rotation to lock. Audible/tactile click at lock position.
MaterialPOM (Delrin®) acetal copolymer — DuPont or equivalent
Weight~2g per clip, ~8g per 4-pack (lighter than magnetic alternatives)
Color options5 Pantone colors: Black, White, Clear, Coral Pink (Pantone 16-1546), Sky Blue (Pantone 14-4122)

Injection Mold Design

Single mold, 2 cavities (base + cap produced simultaneously). Steel mold rated for 100,000+ shots — sufficient for 50,000+ units before mold replacement needed.

Tooling cost: $2,500–$4,000 depending on factory. Tooling is a one-time investment — per-unit cost drops to $0.04–$0.08 amortized over the first 50,000 units.

Color changes during production: POM can be color-changed by purging the injection barrel between colors. Typical changeover: 15–30 minutes. All 5 colors can be produced in a single production run.
