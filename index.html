🏔️ Brand Manifesto & Business Rationale

The Thesis

Every runner knows the ritual. It's 5:30 AM on race morning. You're fumbling with four tiny safety pins, poking your fingers, punching holes in a $120 race jersey. Magnetic clips exist but interfere with RFID timing chips. We built the first mechanical twist-lock alternative — zero magnets, zero pins, zero fabric damage. Insert, twist, click — race.

The name BIVVO comes from "bivouac" — a temporary camp for adventurers. Race morning is your bivouac: the brief, focused moment before the adventure begins.

🎯 Category Gap

$14.99 pinless solution in a market dominated by $8–$15 magnetic clips that risk timing chip interference. First twist-lock mechanism in the category.

🔧 POM Engineering

Delrin® copolymer rated for 500+ races. Same material used in precision mechanical components. Injection-molded in China at $0.50/unit landed.

📦 Amazon-First

85% Year 1 revenue via Amazon FBA. Brand Registry + A+ Content + 8 PPC campaigns. Shopify DTC (5%) and Race Expos (10%) for brand building.

📈 Year 2 Expansion

Walmart Marketplace (3–5%), TikTok Shop (2–4%), multi-color collection strategy. DTC grows to 15–20% as highest-margin channel.
