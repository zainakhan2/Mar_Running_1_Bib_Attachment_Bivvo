📄 Executive Summary

Twist-lock pinless race bib clips for the US running market. Amazon FBA + Shopify DTC. $14.99/4-pack. Zero magnets. Timing chip safe. 500+ race lifespan.

The Problem

Safety pins are the #1 race-morning complaint across running forums (Reddit, LetsRun, Strava clubs). They puncture expensive jerseys ($60–$150), poke fingers, and get dropped in wet grass at 5:30 AM. Magnetic bib clips exist but carry a theoretical risk of interfering with RFID timing chips — a deal-breaker for competitive runners.

No mechanical (non-magnetic) pinless solution exists at consumer scale. BIVVO fills this gap.

The Solution

BIVVO is a twist-lock (bayonet-mount) race bib clip. Insert through bib + fabric, twist 90° clockwise, hear the CLICK — locked. Zero magnets, zero pins, zero fabric damage. POM (Delrin®) copolymer rated for 500+ races. 4-pack, 5 colors, $14.99.
