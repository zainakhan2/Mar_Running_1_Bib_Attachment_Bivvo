About Us

Brand story, etymology, and founder mission.

We Built BIVVO Because We Were Tired of Safety Pins.

Every runner knows the ritual. It's 5:30 AM on race morning. You're standing in a parking lot, fumbling with four tiny safety pins, trying to attach your bib without poking your fingers or punching holes in your favorite race jersey.

We've been there hundreds of times. And one morning, after dropping the third pin into wet grass, we asked: why is this still the way we do this?

BIVVO is the answer. A twist-lock race bib clip that replaces safety pins forever. No magnets (your timing chip stays safe), no holes in your fabric, no fumbling in the dark. Just insert, twist, click — and race.

The name BIVVO comes from "bivouac" — a temporary camp for adventurers. Because race morning is your bivouac: the brief, focused moment before the adventure begins. We wanted that moment to be effortless.

Ditch the pins. Keep the click.
