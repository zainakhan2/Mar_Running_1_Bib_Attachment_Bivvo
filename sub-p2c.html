P2C: Brand Voice & Messaging

Confident, runner-to-runner, warm, slightly playful, never corporate.

Voice Attributes

AttributeDescriptionExample
ConfidentWe know our product works. No hedging.'BIVVO clicks locked. Period.'
Runner-to-runnerWe're runners talking to runners. Peer, not brand.'We've dropped that third pin in wet grass too.'
WarmFriendly, approachable, human.'Happy racing! — The BIVVO Team'
Slightly playfulA wink, not a clown. Subtle humor.'The satisfying click? Chef's kiss.'
Never corporateNo jargon, no 'leveraging synergies.'NOT: 'BIVVO disrupts the bib attachment paradigm.'

Key Messages

Tagline: 'Ditch the Pins. Keep the Click.'

Hashtag: #DitchThePins

Elevator pitch: 'BIVVO is a twist-lock race bib clip that replaces safety pins. No magnets, no holes, no fumbling. Insert, twist, click — race.'

Value proposition hierarchy: (1) Timing chip safe (zero magnets), (2) No fabric damage, (3) 3-second attachment, (4) 500+ race lifespan, (5) 5 colors
