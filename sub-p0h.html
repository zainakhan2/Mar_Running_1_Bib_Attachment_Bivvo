P0H: Legal, Tax & Compliance

Wyoming LLC formation, Pakistan-based founder considerations, Amazon tax compliance, and product safety.

Product Compliance

California Prop 65: POM (Delrin) is generally Prop 65 compliant but testing is recommended for formaldehyde (a known Prop 65 substance that can be a byproduct of POM manufacturing). Cost: $200–$400 per test. If positive, add Prop 65 warning to packaging (reserve zone on insert card per PG-05).

CPSIA: Product is NOT primarily intended for children under 12, so full CPSIA testing is not required. However, recommend labeling 'Ages 13+' as safe harbor. General Certificate of Conformity (GCC) per G-20 resolution.

Amazon compliance: General Certificate of Conformity uploaded to Seller Central. Prop 65 test results on file. Product liability insurance ($500K–$1M policy, $300–$600/yr).

Pakistan-Specific Considerations

  • No US presence required for LLC formation — online via registered agent
  • Payoneer bridges Pakistan banking to US Amazon disbursements
  • W-8BEN eliminates US tax withholding on marketplace sales
  • Pakistan income tax: declare Amazon income as foreign-source business income
  • No double taxation treaty complications — Amazon income is not US-source
  • VPN not required for Seller Central access from Pakistan (Amazon allows global access)
  • Time zone: Pakistan (UTC+5) is well-positioned for US evening customer service
