Naming Convention

Format: [BRAND]-[MECHANISM]-[COLOR]-[PACKSIZE]. BIV=BIVVO, TL=Twist-Lock, 3-letter color code, 4PK=4-pack. Scalable to future SKUs: BIV-TL-RED-4PK, BIV-TL-BLK-8PK, BIV-RB-BLK-1PK (race belt adapter).

GS1 UPCs: 10 purchased ($250). 6 assigned to current SKUs. 4 reserved for Wave 2 expansion (8-pack, new colors, accessories).
