Carton Design

Ready-to-eat (RTE) style carton, 100×70×25mm. Deep Navy background with Electric Coral accents. Die-cut window showing 2 of 4 clips inside. Front: BIVVO logo + 'Twist-Lock Race Bib Clips' + '4-Pack' + color name. Back: 3-step how-to icons + key benefits + UPC barcode.

Material: 350gsm C1S folding carton. Gloss laminate exterior, matte interior. Minimum order: 5,000 (matches first production run). Printed alongside product at factory — single shipment.

Insert Card (per PG-05)

3.5"×2", 350gsm matte coated. FRONT: 3-step diagram (Insert/Twist/Click) with numbered icons. Deep Navy bg, Coral numbers. BACK: QR code (20×20mm) → bivvo.com/welcome, '@bivvo.run | #DitchThePins', TOS-compliant review request, Prop 65 reserve zone.
