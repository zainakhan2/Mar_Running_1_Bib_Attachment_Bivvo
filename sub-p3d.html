Restock Formula

Reorder Point = (Average Daily Sales × Lead Time Days) + Safety Stock

Lead time: 8–12 weeks (factory order to Amazon Available). Safety stock: 14 days of inventory. Example at 5 units/day: reorder when inventory hits (5 × 70) + (5 × 14) = 420 units.

FBM Fallback (G-14): If FBA stock runs out, prep center can ship directly to customers via FBM (Fulfilled by Merchant). Higher fulfillment cost ($5–$7/unit) but prevents lost sales during restock.
