FAQ Page

10 frequently asked questions for bivvo.com/faq.

Q1: How do BIVVO clips work?

Insert through bib + shirt, twist 90° clockwise, click locked. 3 seconds per clip. Twist counter-clockwise to remove. No tools, no damage.

Q2: Will they interfere with my timing chip?

No. Zero magnets = zero magnetic field. RFID timing chips read perfectly. Unlike magnetic clips, BIVVO has no interaction with chip frequencies.

Q3: What fabrics work?

All standard race apparel: tech shirts, cotton, singlets, tri suits, light jackets. Only limitation: very thick winter coats.

Q4: How long do they last?

500+ races. POM (Delrin®) copolymer won't crack, fade, or lose locking strength. A one-time purchase for years of racing.

Q5: Do they damage my shirt?

No. BIVVO passes through existing bib holes and grips fabric without puncturing. Zero marks on clothing.

Q6: What colors are available?

Black, White, Clear (transparent), Coral Pink, Sky Blue. Each sold as a 4-pack. Clear is most versatile; Black is most popular.

Q7: How is BIVVO different from magnetic clips?

Three ways: (1) Timing chip safe (zero magnets), (2) Mechanical retention won't release on slippery fabrics, (3) 500+ race durability vs magnets weakening over time.

Q8: What's your return policy?

30-day hassle-free returns. Defective? Free replacement immediately, no return needed.

Q9: Are these allowed in official races?

Yes. No race rules prohibit bib clips. USATF, World Athletics, all major marathons require visible, secure attachment — not a specific method.

Q10: Where can I buy?

Amazon.com (Prime eligible) or bivvo.com (WELCOME10 for 10% off first order).
