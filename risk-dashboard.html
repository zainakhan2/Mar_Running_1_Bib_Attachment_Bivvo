Critical Playbooks (5)

🚨 Playbook 1: Amazon Account Suspension

Trigger: Account Health drops below 100 or suspension notice received.

  1. Stop all PPC immediately (prevent ad spend on non-buyable ASINs)
  2. Read suspension notice carefully — identify specific policy violation
  3. Draft Plan of Action (POA): root cause → corrective action → preventive measures
  4. Submit appeal via Seller Central within 48 hours
  5. Activate FBM fallback (prep center ships direct) for DTC orders
  6. If denied, escalate to jeff@amazon.com with revised POA

🏭 Playbook 2: Factory Quality Failure

Trigger: QIMA inspection fails AQL 2.5 sampling.

  1. Document all defects with photos and measurements
  2. Send formal rejection notice per P6C PO clause 7
  3. Request factory rework or re-production at factory cost
  4. Schedule re-inspection ($250-$350 additional)
  5. Adjust launch timeline by 2-4 weeks if needed
  6. Consider backup factory for future orders

💸 Playbook 3: Cash Flow Crunch

Trigger: Payoneer balance insufficient for next restock order.

  1. Pause non-essential spending (social ads, new photography)
  2. Reduce PPC to brand defense only (Campaign #6, $3-5/day)
  3. Delay restock by 1-2 weeks — run existing FBA inventory lower
  4. Increase DTC margin by removing any active discounts
  5. Consider smaller restock (2,500 units vs 5,000)

📉 Playbook 4: Sustained Low Demand

Trigger: Below 2 units/day after 60 days on Amazon.

  1. Audit listing: images, title, bullets — compare to top 3 competitors
  2. Run Vine if not already (get to 15+ reviews faster)
  3. Test lower price point ($12.99) for 2 weeks, measure velocity
  4. Increase PPC budget 50% for 2 weeks, measure ACOS impact
  5. Add 2 more influencer creators for external traffic
  6. If still below 2/day at Day 90, evaluate pivot or exit

😰 Playbook 5: Founder Burnout

Trigger: Consistently exceeding 15 hrs/week for 3+ weeks.

  1. Review time allocation vs G-07 budget — identify the overrun category
  2. Automate: batch social content (Sunday), automate email flows, use saved replies
  3. Delegate: prep center handles logistics, Fiverr handles photography
  4. Reduce scope: cut social from 8 posts/week to 5
  5. Take 1 full day off per week — no Seller Central, no DMs
  6. Re-evaluate at monthly P&L: is the business worth the hours?
