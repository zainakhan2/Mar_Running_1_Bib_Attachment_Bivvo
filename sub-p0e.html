$14.99 Validation

Why $14.99 specifically:

  • Below the $15 'psychological barrier' — consumers perceive $14.99 as 'under fifteen dollars'
  • Above the $12 commodity ceiling — signals quality without triggering 'too expensive for clips' objection
  • Matches BibBoards + $5 premium — justified by 'zero magnets, timing chip safe' value proposition
  • 23.1% net margin on Amazon at this price — healthy for FBA business
  • 38.4% net margin on DTC — excellent for Shopify profitability
  • Room for 10–15% promotional discounts (WELCOME10 at $13.49) while maintaining profitability
  • 4-pack perceived value: $3.75 per clip. Comparable to a single safety pin pack ($3–$5) but reusable 500+ times
