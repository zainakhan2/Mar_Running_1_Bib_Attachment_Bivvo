P0G: Supply Chain & Manufacturing

China manufacturing strategy — factory sourcing, POM injection molding, costs, and logistics.

Manufacturing Overview

ParameterSpecification
LocationGuangdong Province, China (injection molding hub)
MaterialPOM (Delrin®) copolymer — acetal resin
ProcessInjection molding, 2-piece assembly (base + cap)
Tooling cost$2,500–$4,000 (steel mold, 100K+ shot life)
Unit cost (FOB)$0.40–$0.60 per 4-pack at 5,000 MOQ
Landed cost (incl. freight + duty)$0.50 per unit (used in all P&L calculations)
MOQ5,000 units (first run)
Production time4–6 weeks after tooling approval
Colors per run5 colors simultaneously (same mold, different resin)

Factory Selection Criteria

  • Must have POM/Delrin injection molding experience (not just ABS/PP)
  • ISO 9001 certified or equivalent quality management system
  • Minimum 3 years export experience to US/EU markets
  • Willing to sign P6C Purchase Order with 10 quality clauses
  • Can produce RTE packaging (cartons + insert cards) alongside product
  • Accepts QIMA or equivalent third-party inspection
  • Communication in English via WeChat/email with <24hr response time

Logistics Chain

Factory → Inspection → Freight → US Prep Center → Amazon FBA

Step 1: QIMA pre-shipment inspection at factory ($250–$350). AQL 2.5 sampling. Step 2: Air freight to US prep center (7–15 days, $800–$1,500 for 5,000 units). Step 3: Prep center applies FNSKU labels + packs into FBA master cartons. Step 4: FBA inbound via Amazon Partnered Carrier. Step 5: Amazon receiving + check-in (3–7 days).

Total lead time: factory order to Amazon Available = 8–12 weeks. Plan 16-week pre-launch timeline to account for delays.
